Hello! I am a master's student in Artificial Intelligence at Korea University, advised by Prof. Heung-Il Suk in the Machine Intelligence Lab (MILab). My research centers on deep learning for neuroimaging and neural signals. I develop methods that learn expressive representations of brain activity from resting-state fMRI and EEG, aiming to advance the diagnosis of neurodevelopmental and psychiatric disorders. Lately, I have been interested in foundation models for brain signals and their translation into clinical practice.

I would be glad to connect. Please feel free to reach out via email.
